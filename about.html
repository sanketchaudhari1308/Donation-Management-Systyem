About HopeShare

Connecting hearts, changing lives - one donation at a time

Our Story

HopeShare was born from a simple idea: technology can make giving easier and more impactful.

Founded in 2023, we started as a small group of developers and social workers who wanted to bridge the gap between generous donors and dedicated NGOs. Today, we've grown into a platform that connects thousands of donors with verified NGOs across the country.

Our mission is to make donating simple, transparent, and effective. Every rupee donated through HopeShare reaches the intended cause, and donors can track exactly how their contributions are making a difference.
